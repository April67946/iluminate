The Word “Iluminate”

A Vocabulary and Meaning

Etymology

From Latin illuminate" from in - ("upon") and lumen meaning "light".

Definition

Illuminate is a word means to light up something or make it clearer or easier to understand.

Denotation

In its literal sense, illuminate refers to:

  • Providing light
  • Making something visible
Example (Denotation):
The streetlights illuminate the road at night.

Connotation

Beyond its literal meaning, illuminate often suggests:

  • Enlightenment
  • Gaining understanding or clarity
Example (Connotation):
The teacher's explanation illminated the difficult lesson.
